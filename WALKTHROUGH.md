# JobFlow User Walkthrough

Welcome to JobFlow, the premium automated "Done-For-You" job seeking hub! This guide provides a detailed walkthrough to operate the platform effectively.

---

## 1. Onboarding & Registration
- Navigate directly to the JobFlow landing page (`/`).
- Admire the cinematic hero section. Scroll down to look at our service-oriented SaaS features, pricing details, and the executive team (Aditya, Sumit, and Chirag).
- Use the **Call-to-Action Buttons**, or type your phone number straight into the top-right OTP gateway. Enter your details to automatically sign-in.
- A simulated authentication sets up your default `AppContext` tracking score and takes you directly to the **Dashboard**.

## 2. Dashboard (`/dashboard`)
Your primary command center. Here, you'll see:
- Quick animated statistics: Total saved jobs, fetched recruiters, messages sent, and your current reply rate percentage.
- The **AI Score Progress Ring**, tracking your current application readiness dynamically.
- A **Activity Feed** of simulated recruiter interaction notifications.
- The **Saved Jobs Panel** (click to be taken directly to the job manager).

## 3. Gathering Real-Time Leads (`/job-scraper`)
- Use the sidebar to enter **Live Job Sync**.
- In the search bar, type the roles you require (e.g., "Frontend Developer Bangalore Remote").
- Press **Sync**. Wait for the engine simulation to run matching algorithms against our aggregated internal dataset to fetch 40+ mock jobs across top Indian organizations.
- When results load, click the **Bookmark** icon to save a role to your "Saved Jobs".

## 4. Extracting the Hiring Managers (`/recruiter-enrichment`)
- Switch to the **Recruiter Enrichment** tab in the sidebar.
- Your saved jobs will populate on the left menu. Select one or multiple.
- Hit **Find Recruiters**. The screen acts as an ingestion console, visually extracting high-level technical directors, hiring managers, and HR heads corresponding to that job.
- You'll see their exact titles, confidence verification scores, and an "Unlocked" status alongside direct phone numbers/emails.

## 5. Sequencing Automated Pitches (`/outreach-creator`)
- Now head to the **Outreach Creator** tab. You're ready to communicate.
- Select your newly unlocked Recruiter from the left list.
- Select a predefined template (from cold outreach emails to LinkedIn InMails). JobFlow's auto-fill context will inject their name, your name, their company, and role seamlessly.
- You can manually edit the message, or click **AI Improve** to append highly personalized "P.S." paragraphs about their company.
- Note the character counters ensuring you stay below LinkedIn connection message limits. 
- Click **Send**.

## 6. Tracking Application Success (`/tracking`)
- Open the **Tracking & Analytics** module.
- Here, visualize your Sent vs. Open vs. Reply rate funnels mapped on professional interactive charts (Recharts). 
- Explore the **Pipeline Kanban**, indicating exactly which recruiters are in the "Applied", "Replied", and "Interviewing" stages.
- Watch your active schedule notifications flag overdue Follow-up actions in the queue based on the calendar sequences you set. 

## 7. Account Configurations (`/settings`)
- Finally, use the **Settings** menu.
- Easily toggle global dark/light modes.
- Drag-and-drop a simulated resume to update your AI Score internally.
- Inspect the integrated modules (LinkedIn, Gmail, Outlook, Instahyre) and OpenAI API keys configured for automated workflows.

_JobFlow automates the entire funnel so you simply show up to the interviews!_
